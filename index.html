About me

Hi there! I'm Claudia Arias, a web developer with a passion for creating visually appealing and highly functional web applications. With a master's degree in Computer Science from Mercy University and a background in Studio Arts from Lehman College, I blend creativity with technical expertise to build unique digital experiences. Over the years, I have honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and other technologies . My journey into web development was fueled by a love for design and a drive to bring ideas to life through code. I've had the opportunity to work on diverse projects, from designing intuitive user interfaces to developing robust backend systems. Beyond coding, I enjoy staying active with outdoor activities, exploring new cuisines, and diving into creative projects like digital illustration and animation. Looking ahead, I'm excited to take on challenging projects that push the boundaries of web development. I'm eager to collaborate with innovative teams and contribute to building impactful solutions that make a difference. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
